My first Hacktoberfest

About me

Hello! I'm Jiin Eom, the front-end developer of South Korea. Besides development, I am interested in various creative activities. I love the media full of interaction and stories, and I plan to make it in the future. If you want to know more about me, visit my Github page!

My experience

Last month, I learned about Hacktoberfest for the first time. But I didn't have any experience in contributing to open source, so I was hesitant to participate. About 5 days ago, I participated in the hands-on event of the Hacktoberfest Seoul Conference and experienced two open-source pull requests for the first time. This was a very new experience for me and made me want to contribute to open source. Next year, I want to be a developer who can contribute professionally to open source and have a more rewarding experience at the Hacktoberfest. Let's meet again next year!
