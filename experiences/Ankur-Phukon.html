My Experience with First Open Source Contribution


About me

I am a ReactJs developer living in India. In this article, I am sharing my first open source contribution.

This year(2020) in Oct, I got to know about Open source societies in Github. I looked for some projects which are working with my knowledgeable area. I found some repositories which are working with ReactJS. After reading the Contribution Doc(Which is provided on the repo), I got interested in adding some new fatures with ReactJs. First I forked and cloned the repository to my account and local machine. Then I created a new branch and stated coding. I developed some information showing related pages where react components are get updated from a Mongo database. After commiting and pushing all that stuff, I pulled a request to the owner of the repo and after some days, my commits were accepted. That's all. Open Source Projects are really good to get an Experience with Real-World Projects. Try it now.

HAPPY READING!
