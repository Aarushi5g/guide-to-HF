My Open Source Experience




Hello fellow mates, my name is Aryan. In my opinion, getting started along with having an interest to keep you consistent
with the work are some of the key tips to keep one intact with the open source community.
There are innumerable amount of materials these days, you just need to have a will to get started.

My journey of open source began from the Hacktober Fest 2019 itself and since then mainly it has taught me various things like uploading your project live to the world,
forking others repo for contributing in their projects which helps in connecting people with same minds around the globe and thus openning door for various oppurtunities.
Moreover it also creates a good impression on the interviewer(if you're giving any) be it undergrad or masters/M.Tech students applying mainly for tech fields. As it is said,
"The smallest of contributions made collectively makes the biggest difference."
